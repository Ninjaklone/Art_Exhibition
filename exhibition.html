Exhibition Overview

The Awards Art Exhibition is an annual event that showcases the best works from talented artists across various mediums. This year, we are excited to present a diverse range of artworks that reflect the creativity and innovation of contemporary art.

Featured Artists

  • Gara Kinkinsoko (ガラキンキンソコ) - Known for his vibrant abstract paintings that explore the themes of nature and emotion.
  • Ezeobi Henry - A sculptor who uses recycled materials to create thought-provoking installations.
  • Ezekiel Joshua - A photographer whose work captures the beauty of urban landscapes.
  • Gbolohan-Lowo Daniel- A renowned mastermind in abstract art and the founder of the Gbolohan Institute of Modern Art.

Event Schedule

  • Opening Reception: March 15, 2024, 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Artist Talks: March 20, 2024, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
  • Workshops: March 25, 2024, 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Closing Ceremony: April 15, 2024, 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Location

The exhibition will be held at the Art Gallery, Downtown. The gallery is easily accessible by public transport and has ample parking available.

Address: 123 Art St, Downtown, City, State, ZIP
